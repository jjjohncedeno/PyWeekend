Designed to meet the unique needs of educational institutions:

  • Collaborate in real-time like you were "there" with anyone, anywhere in the world.
  • Communicate online using the interactive white-board, chat, shared document and webpage viewing, computer screen sharing, and webcam and audio communication.
  • Enjoy unlimited use: meet as often and as long as you want.
  • Remotely work together on class projects.
  • Host a virtual classroom were you can share online lectures.
  • Explain and learn complex subjects using just your web browser.
